## Key Stories
- **The Lie of Loud Leadership**: Greg describes accepting the idea for "far too long" that the only way to stand out is to fake an overconfident, loud personality — and frames the entire podcast as his rejection of that premise and an invitation to others who have lived the same experience.

## Techniques & Frameworks
- **Fight-sized tools**: Greg's framing for the practical, bite-sized confidence, presence, and communication tools the show will deliver — small enough to act on immediately.
- **Introvert Army concept**: A community identity for quiet leaders who are actively reshaping workplace culture from within, rather than conforming to extroverted norms.
