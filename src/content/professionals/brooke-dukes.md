  Brooke M Dukes is the founder of Success by Design, a consultancy that helps established founders and CEOs build organizations that don't depend entirely on their personal involvement to run. Her core insight: most scaling problems aren't people problems or strategy problems — they're systems problems. She works with $1M+ revenue companies to extract the implicit judgment calls living in a founder's head and encode them into decision frameworks, delegation protocols, and operating rhythms the whole organization can run on. She draws on behavioral science and psychology to help leaders communicate authority in ways others can internalize and act on independently.
